What is Project Cheevo?

It's estimated that almost 3.5 billion people around the world play video games of some form. With the extensive range of games and platforms we have today, it's no wonder that just under half the worlds entire population find themselves part of this group.

Along with the progression in games, scores and achievements have also evolved from being the top ten listed scores on an arcade machine leaderboard. Between this evolution and the rise in variety in platforms, at Project Cheevo, we believe it's time for progression recognition and tracking to evolve too.
